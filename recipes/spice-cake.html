Spice Cake


What're We Making?

This is a recipe for Spice Cake from Brian's mom. She gave it to me after I made Pumpkin Bread for them.


Ingredients

  • 3/4 Cup of Shortening
  • 2 1/4 Cup of Flour
  • 1 Cup of Sugar
  • 1 tsp of Baking *Powder*
  • 1 tsp of Salt
  • 3/4 tsp of Baking *Soda*
  • 3/4 tsp of Cinnamon
  • 1/3 tsp of Cloves
  • 3/4 Cup of Brown Sugar
  • 1 Cup of Buttermilk
  • 3 Eggs

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease your desired pans, with either butter or shortening, and set aside.
  2. Combine all ingredients into a bowl and mix well.
  3. Add the remaining ingredients and mix until well combined.
  4. Pour the batter into your prepared pans, and bake at 350 for 30 minutes, or until you stab a fork in the middle and it comes out clean.
  5. Allow bread to cool for 15 minutes in the pan, then transfer on a rack to finish cooling
  6. Serve with your toppings of choice! Enjoy!
